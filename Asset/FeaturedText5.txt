Gujarati Thali is an assortment of dishes like ringana methi nu shak (eggplant cooked with fenugreek leaves), Gujarati khatti mithi daal (sweet and sour lentil), undhiyu (mixed vegetable prepared with spinach paste), steamed basmati rice, badshahi khichdi and bhakhri with kachumber saladalong with various types of chutneys.
